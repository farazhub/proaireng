Why Choose Us

Global Partnerships for Superior Solutions

Pro Air Engineering was formed in 1985 in Lahore, Pakistan and has earned a reputable image. Pro Air Engineering provides all kinds of services for refrigeration air dryers, Air compressors (oil injected & oil free) and compressed air filters of international brands. The CEO has an experience of over 38+ years in Compressed Air technology. PAE has seen tremendous growth over the years and aims to provide the best products and services to its valued customers. We are pleased to announce that PAE has also launched a sister company “HS Technologies”.

  • DINLON TECH
    Fujian Dinlon Technology, a Chinese compressor manufacturer, provides global air system solutions. Their products include Rotary Screw Air Dryers, Compressors and accessories serving diverse industries.
  • Fusheng
    Since 1953, Fusheng, a Taiwanese industry leader, offers diverse air compressors and solutions, optimizing efficiency, cutting costs, and promoting environmental responsibility worldwide.
  • Sotras
    Founded in 1975, Sotras Srl is a leading industrial filter brand offering high-quality products in Europe and worldwide. They specialize in filters. Pro Air Engineering has been their official distributor since 2003.
